## Section B — Quests & weapon to knock out **now** (no SoA gate)

These need only level 99 (which you have) — do them immediately; none wait on the story.

### B1. Dimidiation — the great-sword weaponskill
- **Quest "Rune Fencing the Night Away," Gaddiux (Western Adoulin, J-10).** Get a **Trial Blade**, build
  WS points (use a **Light** skillchain — e.g. **Trust: Ayame** for Resolution → Light, farm in **Kamihr
  Drifts**), then trade it back.
- **Why first:** Dimidiation is RUN's "tank while still contributing damage" WS — you want it before you
  start tanking real content.

### B2. Aettir (your tank great sword) — it's an **Oboro augment, not a Magian grind**
▶ **Correction:** an earlier draft called Aettir a Magian-trial weapon. It isn't. Aettir is an
**Oboro-augmented** iL119 great sword, and the "~300 Pluton/Beitetsu/Riftborn Boulder" is the **Oboro
augment cost**, not a trial chain.
- **Prereq:** the **Scintillating Rhapsody** key item — **you already have it** (from RoV, which you've
  completed). So Oboro's augment menu is open to you now.
- **Innate tank stats:** **Enmity +10, Physical Damage Taken II −5%**, iL119, Great Sword skill +242,
  Magic Acc skill +188.
- **The augment:** trade **300× a single material** to **Oboro (Port Jeuno)** for the max augment
  **Accuracy +70 / Magic Evasion +50 / Weapon Skill Damage +10%**. The **+50 Magic Evasion** is the tank
  draw. Pick the **cheapest material — Pluton**:

  | Material | Bahamut median | ×300 | 
  |----------|----------------|------|
  | **Pluton** (use this) | ~1,000 | **~300,000 gil** |
  | Riftborn Boulder | ~2,000 | ~600,000 |
  | Beitetsu | ~4,000 | ~1,200,000 |

- **Bottom line:** ~**300k gil** + the base Aettir gets you a fully-augmented tank GS — cheap and
  high-impact. **[CONFIRM the exact source of the un-augmented base Aettir in-game]** (Oboro/vendor).
- Use an **Ambuscade great sword** as a zero-effort floor until Aettir is augmented.

### B3. Acquire the base armor sets (the "Rune quests")
RUN's three JSE sets come from **quests** for the iL109 base, then ride the reforge ladder. Get the

- **Erilaz (Empyrean) — THE core tank set; get this first.** Quest **"Epiphany"**: talk to

  **"Futharkic Concepts in Flux"** key item (Hestefa/Amchuchu questline) and the Sagheera relic-armor
