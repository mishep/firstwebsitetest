# FFXI Progression Guides

Personal, account-specific Final Fantasy XI endgame progression guides. Three cross-referenced
documents that take a single account from its current state toward two goals: a very strong solo
**Blue Mage** and a functional **Rune Fencer** tank.

## The guides

| Guide | What it covers |
|-------|----------------|
| **[ACCOUNT_PROGRESSION.md](./ACCOUNT_PROGRESSION.md)** | Daily/weekly activities, priority ordering, what each one produces, and a gil-making analysis. The "what do I do today" guide. |
| **[BLU_PROGRESSION.md](./BLU_PROGRESSION.md)** | **Primary goal** — sequenced plan to take every BLU JSE piece rated 7/10+ to Reforged +2, with per-piece requirement chains, gates, spells, and trust setups. |
| **[RUN_PROGRESSION.md](./RUN_PROGRESSION.md)** | **Secondary goal** — Rune Fencer tank leveling (81→99), unlock quests, and tank gear from the Sparks floor through reforged JSE and Nyame. |

## How they fit together

The three guides are heavily cross-referenced: each daily activity points to the goal it feeds, and
each gear target points back to the activity that supplies its inputs. A good reading order is
**ACCOUNT → BLU → RUN**.

```
ACCOUNT_PROGRESSION  ──(activities feed)──►  BLU_PROGRESSION (primary)
        │                                            ▲
        └──────────(shared gates/unlocks)───────►  RUN_PROGRESSION (secondary)
```

## Notes

- Built from a detailed account brief and verified against current (mid-2026) community sources
  (BG-wiki, FFXIAH, We Are Vana'diel) where reachable.
- Version-volatile numbers (reforge costs, card counts, currency amounts) are flagged
  **`[CONFIRM in-game]`** with the NPC that is the live source of truth, since FFXI's endgame meta
  shifts with version updates.
- These guides are tailored to one specific account's state, jobs, and trust roster — treat the
  sequencing as account-specific rather than universal best practice.
