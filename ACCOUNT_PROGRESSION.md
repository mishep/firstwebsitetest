## The daily/weekly priority order

Do the **time-gated** things first so a regenerating timer is never sitting wasted. Everything below
the gated block is "fill" you can do any time.

1. **Ambuscade** — daily seal bonus (resets daily). *Never skip.*
2. **Omen** — burn a Mystical Canteen if one is charged (~20 hr regen).
3. **Sortie** — once per Earth day per character (~20 hr). *(Unlock first — trivial; see below.)*
4. **Odyssey** — burn a Moglophone charge (~20 hr regen). **Bring Valaineral + Reraise.**
5. **Domain Invasion** — only if a wave is currently active on your server.
6. **Fill / open-ended:** Reisenjima NMs & Geas Fete (gil + spells + Rem's Tale chapters).

**Background progression tracks** (not daily — chip away whenever):
- **SoA mission questing** (2-7-1 → ~5-3-2) to unlock Vagary. *This unblocks the most BLU work — treat
  it as the single most important non-daily project.*
- **Normal Dynamis farming** (Beaucedine/Tavnazia) for the 4 missing Mirage relic pieces, which also
  walks you toward the Dynamis-Tavnazia boss that unlocks Dynamis-Divergence.

## Activity reference

### 1. Ambuscade — the non-negotiable daily
- **What:** rotating monthly fight, two difficulties (Normal/Very Difficult) with adjustable tiers.
- **Gate:** RoE "Stepping into an ambuscade" then talk to **Gorpa-Masorpa (Mhaura, G-9)**. Already
  unlocked on your account.
- **Cooldown:** repeatable all day; the **first clear each day gives 3× hallmark/badge** (daily seal).
- **Produces:** Hallmarks & Badges → traded to Gorpa-Masorpa for **Ambuscade capes** (and their
  augments via Abdhaljs materials), plus sellable materials and useful consumables.
- **Feeds:**
  - **RUN tank** → the **Ogma's Cape** built DT+enmity is RUN's single best easy-to-get piece. See
    [`RUN_PROGRESSION.md` → Section C, item 2](./RUN_PROGRESSION.md#section-c--tank-gear-no-run-armor-yet).
  - **BLU** → its own augmented cape and capacity/utility items.
  - **Gil** → surplus hallmark trades and material drops.
- **Why first:** lowest effort, highest guaranteed daily value, and the only source of the augmented
  capes both jobs want.

### 2. Omen — Artifact +2 fuel (BLU)
- **What:** instanced content in Reisenjima; you pick a "scion" path. For your goal you want the
  **small-fry / "light" card-farming path, NOT the bosses.**
- **Gate:** **Mystical Canteen** (made via the Incantrix synth / obtained through Ethereal Ingress #10,
  Reisenjima). Unlocked. **~20 hr canteen regen; stockpile 3–4 so you never waste the timer.**
- **Produces:** **Paragon Job Cards** (BLU cards) → the currency for **Reforged Artifact +2/+3**, plus
  Omen scales (needed at +3) and sellable drops.
- **Feeds:** **BLU Artifact line** — see [`BLU_PROGRESSION.md` → Phase 2](./BLU_PROGRESSION.md#phase-2--artifact-body-is-the-prize).
  Your AF +2 targets are **Body (10/10)** and **Head (7/10)**; each piece needs **6–10 BLU cards**
  ([CONFIRM exact per-slot count in-game] — varies by slot). Budget roughly **12–20 cards** for both,
  i.e. a handful of efficient runs.
- **Why BLU is ideal here:** self-skillchain + magic burst lets you clear card mobs solo faster than
  most jobs. Run the **carry trust set** from [`BLU_PROGRESSION.md`](./BLU_PROGRESSION.md#trust-setups).
- **Note:** you can also exchange unwanted job cards for BLU cards at **Coelestrox (Reisenjima)** at a
  **5:1** ratio if you over-farm the wrong job.

### 3. Sortie — Empyrean +2/+3 fuel + the Empyrean earring (BLU)
- **What:** large timed instance under Ra'Kaznar.
- **Gate to unlock (do once, trivial):** get the **shiny Ra'Kaznarian plate from Ruspix (Leafallia,
  H-8)**, then enter at the **Kamihr Drifts Bivouac #4 Diaphanous Transposer.** Do this *now* — it's
  the fastest unblock in your whole plan. **[CONFIRM the entry NPC/coords in-game.]**
- **Cooldown:** **once per Earth day per character (~20 hr).**
- **Produces:** **Gallimaufry** (Empyrean upgrade currency), **Ra'Kaznar Sapphires/Starstones**, and
  **Old Cases** (which can yield the **Empyrean earring**, BiS magic-acc for BLU).
- **Feeds:** **BLU Empyrean line** — see [`BLU_PROGRESSION.md` → Phase 1](./BLU_PROGRESSION.md#phase-1--empyrean-the-crown-jewel).
  Confirmed costs: **+2 = 10,000 Gallimaufry + 1 Ra'Kaznar Sapphire per piece; +3 = 70,000 Gallimaufry
  + 1 Ra'Kaznar Starstone per piece** (traded to **Ruspix**).
- **Also feeds RUN:** the **Erilaz** tank pieces (reforged Empyrean) take the **same Sortie +2 path** —
  see [`RUN_PROGRESSION.md` → Section C, item 4](./RUN_PROGRESSION.md#section-c--tank-gear-no-run-armor-yet).
- **Important nuance:** Gallimaufry is **capped at 100,000 held** until you finish *The Voracious
  Resurgence* missions. That's enough to bank toward several +2s now, but you can't hoard for the big
  +3 (70k) pushes until that cap is lifted. **[CONFIRM current cap in-game.]**
- **Dependency to remember:** the Sortie +2 *step* has **no Vagary requirement**, but the
  **109→119 step that precedes it does** (see Vagary, below). So: **bank Sortie currency now**, but you
  can't finish a piece to +2 until it's already 119, which needs Vagary titles.

### 4. Dynamis-Divergence — Relic +2/+3 fuel (BLU)
- **What:** the upgraded Dynamis. Visit the **zone matching the relic piece while on BLU** to upgrade.
- **Gate to unlock:** defeat the **Dynamis-Tavnazia zone boss** + get an **Empty Hourglass from Aurix
  (Ru'Lude Gardens, G-8)**. You have RoV done (one prereq). **Not yet unlocked.**
- **Cooldown:** **~60 hr (about 2.5 days) lockout** per entry. **[CONFIRM current lockout in-game.]**
- **Produces:** Relic reforge progress (+2 from visiting the matching zone with the job; +3 from
  mid-boss kills / 100-mob clears) plus **Ancient Currency**, a reliable gil source.
- **Feeds:** **BLU Relic line** — see [`BLU_PROGRESSION.md` → Phase 3](./BLU_PROGRESSION.md#phase-3--relic-feet--body-acquire-then-reforge).
  Also the **RUN Futhark** tank pieces ride this same Dyna-D path.
- **How it unlocks naturally:** you need to farm **normal Dynamis** anyway for your 4 missing Mirage
  relic base pieces (see Background Tracks). Killing the Tavnazia boss along the way flips on Dyna-D.

### 5. Odyssey — long-term Nyame + a strong gil source (both jobs)
- **What:** Sheol A / B / C wings; later Sheol Gaol bosses (Bumba) for the best gear.
- **Gate:** **Moglophone** from the **Pilgrim Moogle (Rabao, G-6)**. Unlocked. **~20 hr regen.**
- **⚠️ Survival:** Odyssey mobs are **Content Level 130+** — you died to crawlers your first run.
  **Always bring Valaineral (tank trust) + keep Reraise up.** Use the **Odyssey survivability trust
  set** in [`BLU_PROGRESSION.md`](./BLU_PROGRESSION.md#trust-setups).
- **Produces:** Moogle Segments (buy buffs/keys), **Lustreless Remnants** in Sheol C (the **Nyame**
  material path), and **gil** (every Nostos enemy adds **+5,000 gil in A / +6,000 in B / +7,000 in C**
  to your reward; **Lustreless drops stack to 99 and sell ~1M each**).
- **Feeds:**
  - **RUN tank** → **Nyame** is the endgame defensive base set. See
    [`RUN_PROGRESSION.md` → Section C, item 5](./RUN_PROGRESSION.md#section-c--tank-gear-no-run-armor-yet).
  - **Gil** → one of your best per-hour sources (see **Gil Engine**).
  - BLU long-term → Nyame is also a strong BLU defensive/hybrid base.
- **Plan:** run **Sheol A/B** now for segments, gil, and safety; graduate to **Sheol C** for Nyame mats
  as your RUN tank set comes online. Gaol/Bumba (best Nyame) is a group activity for later.

### 6. Limbus — bank it for later (+4, far future)
- **What:** Apollyon (→ Artifact **+4**) and Temenos (→ Relic **+4**).
- **Gate:** CoP + Scintillating Rhapsody — both done. **No time limit. Up to 3 trusts.**
- **Produces:** Units (the +4 currency).
- **Feeds:** **BLU +4** — but **+4 requires the piece already be +3.** You have nothing at +3 yet, so
  Limbus is not on your critical path.
- **Plan for now:** make **one** trip to grab the **verification keys** and the **one-time free
  Alabaster / Murky Matter**, then **deprioritize** until your first pieces hit +3. Use the **3-trust
  Limbus set** in [`BLU_PROGRESSION.md`](./BLU_PROGRESSION.md#trust-setups).

### 7. Domain Invasion — opportunistic
- **What:** server-scheduled open-world campaign.
- **Gate:** none beyond it being active.
- **Produces:** **Escha Beads** (used to pay reforge *delivery fees* at Coelestrox/Monisette — e.g.
  10 beads = piece ready next game-day, 500 = instant for AF +2) and the **Iris** great club (a fine
  BLU off-option). Also worthwhile XP/currency.
- **Feeds:** keeps a bead stock so reforge deliveries are never blocked. Tag it whenever it's live.

### 8. Reisenjima NMs / Geas Fete — your engine room
- **What:** open-world tier-1/2/3 NMs in Reisenjima via **Geas Fete** (trade the matching stone).
- **Gate:** none (RoV done).
- **Produces:** **the best gil on your account** (NM mats sell high), **Rem's Tale chapters**, and
  several **BLU AoE spells** (Heavenly-series nukes) — a triple-dip.
- **Feeds:** **Gil Engine** (below), BLU spell completion (see
  [`BLU_PROGRESSION.md` → Spell sidebar](./BLU_PROGRESSION.md#spell-completion-sidebar)), and the
  reforge chapter supply (you already hold 12 of each, so this is bonus).
- **Why it matters most for you:** BLU is the *best* job in the game for this — **Abduction** pulls
  packs, Gravity locks them, AoE nukes delete them. This is how you solve the **under-5M-gil** problem.

### 9. Vagary — blocked behind SoA (the key BLU gate)
- **What:** instanced battles that grant **Mega Boss titles**.
- **Gate:** **SoA Mission ~5-3-2 "Blood for Blood"** (you're at 2-7-1, Chapter 2 — **a full chapter+
  away**) **plus** the **Prototype Sigil Pearl** (trade **Befouled Water** — dropped by Fomors in Outer
  Ra'Kaznar / Ra'Kaznar Inner Court — to the **Odyssean Passage in Leafallia**; also AH-buyable under
  *Other > Misc #2*) and a Leafallia cutscene flag.
- **Produces:** **Vagary Mega Boss titles**, which are **required for the 109→119 reforge step on both
  Artifact and Empyrean** (Relic does **not** need them).
- **Feeds:** **everything 119+ on Artifact and Empyrean for BLU**, and the **Erilaz/AF-based RUN tank
  pieces** too. This is why **finishing SoA is the highest-leverage non-daily task on your account.**
- **Status:** **LOCKED.** Prioritize the SoA questing track to open it.

### 10. SoA Mission questing — the unlock project (not a daily)
- **What:** push the Seekers of Adoulin story from **2-7-1 → ~5-3-2**.
- **Why:** it's the prerequisite for **Vagary** (#9), which gates the **119 step** for AF + Empyrean.
- **How:** these are largely solo-able with your trust roster (use the carry set). Chip away between
  daily timers. **This is your "long pole" — start it immediately and keep moving it.**

## The Gil Engine — solving the under-5M problem

You have **under ~5M gil**, and your biggest gil sink is **Artifact +2 synthesis materials at
~1,126,125 gil per piece** ([CONFIRM at Guild Vendors / Coelestrox] — this is the verified current
figure but it can shift). With 2 AF pieces targeted (Body + Head), that's **~2.25M in synth mats
alone**, before consumables. So gil farming is a real, near-term track — not an afterthought.

**Ranked for *your* profile (solo BLU 99, strong, limited session time):**

| Method | Why it ranks here | Rough return | Notes |
|--------|-------------------|--------------|-------|
| **1. Reisenjima NM / Geas Fete farming (BLU)** | BLU is the best job for it; no gate, no timer, scales with your gear | High & steady; NM mats are the bread-and-butter | **Your primary gil source.** Triple-dips with spells + chapters. Treasure Hunter helps. |
| **2. Odyssey Sheol A/B/C** | Time-gated but you're running it anyway; gil rides along with Nyame/segment goals | +5k–7k gil *per Nostos kill* + **Lustreless stacks selling ~1M** | Already on your daily list — pure bonus gil. Bring the tank trust. |
| **3. Dynamis (normal + Divergence)** | You're farming it for Mirage pieces anyway | **Ancient Currency** sells reliably | Gil rides along with the Relic-piece farm. |
| **4. Sortie** | Daily you're doing anyway | Upgrade mats + occasional high-value drops | Treat gil as a side effect, not the reason. |
| **5. Ambuscade** | Daily you're doing anyway | Surplus hallmark trades + mats | Small but free. |

**Recommended gil routine:** lean on **Reisenjima/Geas Fete as your dedicated gil block**, and let
**Odyssey + Dynamis + Sortie + Ambuscade** contribute gil as a byproduct of activities you're already
running for gear. That covers the ~2.25M AF synth bill without a separate grind eating your sessions.
**Don't** chase Artifact +2 until you've banked enough that the synth mats won't drain you to zero —
sequence it after a couple of good Reisenjima sessions. **[CONFIRM per-hour rates in-game]** — current
sources rank Reisenjima (BLU) and Odyssey as the top endgame solo gil; precise gil/hour isn't published.
