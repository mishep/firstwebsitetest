## The reforge ladder (how every piece moves)

```
Base armor ──► 109 ──► 119 ("+1") ──► +2 ──► +3 ──► +4
              Monisette  Monisette     set-     set-    Limbus
              (Port Jeuno I-8)         specific specific (Units)
```

- **109 and 119 steps: Monisette (Port Jeuno, I-8).** Needs **Rem's Tale chapters + per-slot
  materials.** You hold **12 of every chapter (1–10)** → fully covered. Reforged piece is ready the
  next Vana'diel day.
- **The 109→119 step has extra gates by set:**
  - **Artifact & Empyrean:** require the **Vagary Mega Boss title** for that slot. → **gated by SoA
    questing** (see [`ACCOUNT_PROGRESSION.md` → Vagary](./ACCOUNT_PROGRESSION.md#9-vagary--blocked-behind-soa-the-key-blu-gate)).
  - **Empyrean additionally:** **1 Isgebind's Heart per piece** (Abyssea-Uleguerand). **You hold 4+ —
    effectively covered** ([CONFIRM you have 1 per remaining piece; an H-P Bayld alt exists if short]).
  - **Relic:** **no Vagary title, no Heart** — only chapters + materials.
- **The +2 step differs by set** (see each phase below).
- **+4** (Limbus) requires the piece to already be **+3** — far future, not on your critical path.

**Per-slot reforge materials** are itemized with current Bahamut prices in the next section.

## Phase 0 — Prerequisites (start all of these in parallel, today)

These are the slow unlocks that gate the fast reforge steps. Kick them off immediately.

1. **Unlock Sortie** — grab the plate from **Ruspix (Leafallia, H-8)**. Trivial; opens the entire
   Empyrean +2 path. Then bank Gallimaufry daily.
   → [`ACCOUNT_PROGRESSION.md` → Sortie](./ACCOUNT_PROGRESSION.md#3-sortie--empyrean-23-fuel--the-empyrean-earring-blu).
2. **▶ THE LONG POLE: SoA questing 2-7-1 → ~5-3-2** to unlock **Vagary**. Vagary titles are required
   for **every 109→119 step on Artifact and Empyrean** — i.e. you cannot finish your highest-value
   pieces without this. **Nothing else unblocks more. Start now, keep moving it.**
   → [`ACCOUNT_PROGRESSION.md` → SoA / Vagary](./ACCOUNT_PROGRESSION.md#10-soa-mission-questing--the-unlock-project-not-a-daily).
3. **Normal Dynamis farm** for the **4 missing Mirage relic pieces** (head/body/hands/feet base), and
   kill the **Dynamis-Tavnazia boss** to unlock **Dynamis-Divergence** (the Relic +2 path).
   → [`ACCOUNT_PROGRESSION.md` → Dynamis-D](./ACCOUNT_PROGRESSION.md#4-dynamis-divergence--relic-23-fuel-blu).
4. **Isgebind's Hearts — essentially done.** You hold **4+**, Abyssea is unlocked. You need **1 per
   Empyrean piece** for its 109→119 step. There are 5 Empyrean armor slots, so **confirm you have a 5th
   Heart**; if short by one, it's a single quick Abyssea-Uleguerand run (Drake → Kur → Isgebind
   influence chain), or use the **H-P Bayld** alternative. Not a blocker.

**Sequencing truth to internalize:** you can **bank Sortie Gallimaufry and Omen cards right now**, but
you **cannot complete a piece to +2 until it is 119**, and the **119 step waits on Vagary** (except
Relic). So Phase 0's SoA push is what converts your banked currency into finished gear.

## Phase 1 — Empyrean (the crown jewel)

**Why first:** four **10/10** pieces + one **8/10** + a **9/10** earring. This is the highest
concentration of value on your account, and the +2 currency (Sortie) is one of your easiest dailies.

### Per-piece chains
| Piece | From → to | Needs |
|-------|-----------|-------|
| **Mavi Tayt → Hashishin (legs, 10)** | base → 109 → 119 | chapters + mats; **then** 119 needs **Vagary legs title + 1 Isgebind's Heart** |
| Hashishin Kavuk (head, 10) | 109 → 119 → +2 | **Vagary head title + Heart** (119); then **10k Gallimaufry + 1 Sapphire** (+2) |
| Hashishin Mintan (body, 10) | 109 → 119 → +2 | as above (body title) |
| Hashishin Bazubands (hands, 10) | 109 → 119 → +2 | as above (hands title) |
| Hashishin Basmak (feet, 8) | 109 → 119 → +2 | as above (feet title) |
| **Empyrean earring (9)** | acquire | from **Sortie Old Cases** |

### Sequenced steps
1. **Reforge Mavi Tayt → Hashishin 109** at Monisette (chapters + mats). Do this now — it's the only
   un-reforged Empyrean slot and doesn't need Vagary yet at the 109 step. *(The 119 step will need the
   legs title + a Heart, like the others.)*
2. **Bank Gallimaufry** from daily Sortie immediately (you can hold up to 100k until *Voracious
   Resurgence* lifts the cap).
3. **Acquire the Empyrean earring** from **Sortie Old Cases** opportunistically as you run Sortie.
4. **When Vagary opens (after SoA ~5-3-2):** push all five Empyrean pieces **109 → 119** at Monisette
   (Vagary title + Isgebind's Heart per piece — both covered or quick).
5. **Then 119 → +2 at Ruspix:** **10,000 Gallimaufry + 1 Ra'Kaznar Sapphire per piece.** Order by
   rating: the four 10/10s first, then Basmak (8).
6. **+3 later (optional):** **70,000 Gallimaufry + 1 Ra'Kaznar Starstone per piece** at Ruspix. This is
   beyond the stated "to +2" goal — pursue only after the whole set is +2 and the Gallimaufry cap is
   lifted.

**Gate summary:** Sortie +2 step is **not** Vagary-gated, but the **119 step is** — so Empyrean
finishes *with* the SoA/Vagary unlock, fueled by currency you banked beforehand.

## Phase 3 — Relic (Feet & Body: acquire, then reforge)

**Why third:** the two best pieces (**Feet 10/10**, **Body 8/10**) **aren't owned yet** — they require
a normal-Dynamis farm first, which is the slowest input. **Relic has no Vagary gate**, so once you have
the base pieces and Dyna-D access, it flows independently of SoA.

### Per-piece chains
| Piece | State | Path | Needs |
|-------|-------|------|-------|
| **Mirage Charuqs → Luhlaza (feet, 10)** | **not owned** | farm → 109 → 119 → +2 | drop from **normal Dynamis**; chapters + mats (no Vagary/Heart); +2 via **Dyna-D** |
| **Mirage Jubbah → Luhlaza (body, 8)** | **not owned** | farm → 109 → 119 → +2 | same |
| **Luhlaza Shalwar +1 (legs, 7)** | **119** ✓ | **119 → +2** | **Dyna-D** only — closest Relic piece to done |
| Relic Head (5) / Hands (1) | not owned / 109 | — | **SKIP** |

### Sequenced steps
1. **Farm Mirage Charuqs (feet) and Mirage Jubbah (body)** from normal Dynamis (the same farm that
   unlocks Dyna-D via the Tavnazia boss).
2. **Reforge each to 119** at Monisette (chapters + mats; **no Vagary title, no Heart** — Relic is the
   easy set mechanically).
3. **+2 via Dynamis-Divergence:** visit the matching zone **on BLU**. **Luhlaza Shalwar (legs) is
   already 119 → push it to +2 first** as your quickest Relic win once Dyna-D is open.
4. **+3 later (optional):** Dyna-D mid-boss kills / 100-mob clears. Beyond the "to +2" goal.

## Recommended completion order (the whole plan, sequenced)

1. **Today:** unlock Sortie; start SoA questing; start normal-Dynamis Mirage farm; confirm a 5th
   Isgebind's Heart; begin Reisenjima gil banking.
2. **Bank in parallel:** Sortie Gallimaufry, Omen BLU cards, gil.
3. **Reforge Mavi Tayt → Hashishin 109** (no gate at the 109 step).
4. **Artifact Body → +2, then Head → +2** (no Vagary gate; gated only by gil/cards) — your fastest
   high-value finishes after a couple of gil sessions.
5. **Push Luhlaza Shalwar (legs) → +2** once Dyna-D opens (quickest Relic win).
6. **When SoA hits ~5-3-2 → Vagary opens:** take all Empyrean **109→119**, then **+2 via Sortie**
   (four 10/10s, then the 8). Acquire the earring along the way.
7. **Finish Relic Feet + Body** (farm → reforge → Dyna-D +2).
8. **Optional beyond-goal:** any +3 pushes, then +4 via Limbus once pieces are +3.
